What can I do with Cops app?

Improve and Suggest for changes around, Start a Movement, Report Abuse, Violations, Traffic Violations, Rash Driving, Wrong Parking, Wrong Sense, Call for Help for People around. Report for Garbage around, Report for Traffic Jams, Report for chance of Improvements, Report & suggest for a Better City tomorrow.

What do I get for Contributing?

More Contributions get you more Karma. The Cops App is powered by a Karma Reward engine(Gamification engine) to reward outstanding contributors in the city with Badges, Karma Scores and Levels.

What Happens when I Report an Activity?

Once you Report an Activity in your Area/City the report is passed to the concerned Authority - City Operator/Mayor/Backend Team.And efforts are made from our Team to take Action immediately.
